🔍 Feed Scout

Scheduled: 2x daily (8am + 6pm) · Manual: "Scan Now"
Scans Gmail for LinkedIn and Jobright job alerts. Parses structured data, deduplicates across sources, and scores using the 7-dimension engine (networkFit weighted at 30%).
Reads: Gmail MCP pf_preferences pf_linkedin_network
Writes: pf_feed_queue pf_feed_runs

🎯 Pipeline Orchestrator

Browser-side: on page load + user actions
Manages role lifecycle through kanban stages. Detects stale roles (7-14 day thresholds), validates stage transitions, and triggers Resume Tailor and Outreach Drafter when roles move to "Applying" or "Reaching Out".
Reads/Writes: pf_roles pf_feed_queue
Triggers: resume-requests/ outreach-requests/

📄 Resume Tailor

Scheduled: every 15 min (polls queue)
Generates tailored one-page PDF resumes using the bullet bank and SKILL.md honesty rules. Creates DOCX with python-docx, converts to PDF via LibreOffice. Every claim validated against 5 honesty checks.
Reads: resume-requests/ pf_roles SKILL.md
Writes: generated-resumes/

✉️ Outreach Drafter

Scheduled: every 15 min (polls queue)
Generates personalized outreach messages across 5 templates (LinkedIn connect, InMail, cold email, referral email, referral ask). Uses real proof points only. Enforces platform character limits.
Reads: outreach-requests/ pf_roles pf_linkedin_network
Writes: outreach-requests/ (updates in-place)

🩺 Health Monitor

Scheduled: every 30 min
Validates all agents, data integrity (JSON parsing, wrapper format), feed freshness, stuck resume requests, stale pipeline roles, and score drift. Produces actionable recommendations.
Reads: All .pathfinder-data/ files (read-only)
Writes: pf_health_report
